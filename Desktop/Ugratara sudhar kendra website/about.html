About Us

Helping individuals reclaim their lives from addiction.

Ugratara Sudhar Kendra is committed to providing comprehensive rehabilitation services for individuals struggling with drug and alcohol addiction. Our mission is to support individuals through their journey to recovery and help them build a positive, drug-free future. With experienced professionals and a compassionate approach, we guide our clients towards a better life, offering both inpatient and outpatient services tailored to individual needs.
